Research Glossary

Quantitative Research

Research that uses numerical data and statistical methods to answer questions and test hypotheses.

Correlational Research

A non-experimental research method that examines the statistical relationship between two or more variables.

Respondents

The individuals who participate in a research study by providing data through surveys, interviews, or observations.

Sampling

The process of selecting a subset of individuals from a population to represent the entire group in research.

Statement of the Problem

A clear and concise description of the issue that the research aims to address or solve.

Research Gap

An area or aspect of a topic that has not been adequately studied or addressed in existing research.

Research Method

The systematic plan or procedure used to collect and analyze data in a research study.
